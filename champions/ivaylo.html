Ivaylo is the Aspect of the Protector, wielding incredible power as guardian of life, love, and beauty. Shamed by a dereliction of duty and exiled from his hometown, Ivaylo ascended ASP.NET to find redemption, only to discover a higher calling among the stars. Imbued with the might of ancient Ivo, the Pro Coder now stands ever vigilant against the boilerplate code.
