Angel Miladinov

Role:

Trainer

Race:

Line:

"Lady luck is smilin'."

Angel once said 'It's not gambling if you can't lose...'' Angel is an infamous card sharp and swindler who has gambled and charmed his way across much of the known world, earning the enmity and admiration of the rich and foolish alike. He rarely takes things seriously, greeting each day with a mocking smile and an insouciant swagger. In every possible way, Angel always has an ace up his sleeve. Born to the nomadic river-folk of the Serpentine, the boy was taught the magic of the cards at an early age. His elders claimed this was the way of the world, but their refusal to fight back against this prejudice always rankled the young boy's code.
