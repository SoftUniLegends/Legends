Simeon Sheytanov

Role: Trainer

Race:

Champion: Warwick

Description: the Code Hunter

Line: "Code runs... they all run."

Sheytanov was once a man revered for his ability to track down coders for the darkest types of technology research. When his ambitions exceeded his physical limits, he drank a dangerous C2H5OH elixir to transform himself into an unstoppable code monster. As his reputation grew, so did the demands placed upon him. His clients wanted more rare and dangerous software products, and they wanted them sooner.
