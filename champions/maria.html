Maria Hristoforova

Role:

Trainer

Race:

Line:

"Let's design it up!"

She grew up as the family's only daughter, and she immediately took to the advanced education and lavish parties required of families as high profile as the Code Fighters. As Maria matured, it became clear that she was extraordinarily gifted. She could design tricks that made people believe they had seen things that did not actually exist. She could also hide in plain sight. Somehow, she was able to reverse engineer arcane magical designs after seeing them made only once.

As one of the youngest women to be tested by the Secret College of Coders, she was discovered to possess a unique abilities over the powers of web. The young Maria viewed this as a great gift, something for her to embrace and use in the name of good. Realizing her unique skills, SoftUni recruited and trained her in covert operations. She quickly became renowned for her daring achievements.
