Svetlin Galov

Role:

Trainer

Race:

C#

Line:

"Live and die by my Code."

Svetlin's earliest memories are the darkness of underground computer clubs. He remembers no family, warmth, or kindness. Kept alive only by his quick keyboard writing, Svetlin scraped out a living in the seedy underbelly of Sofia. His mastery of the binary code quickly marked him as a threat, and SoftUni guilds sent assassins to him with a demand: join their ranks or be killed. Svetlin chose life, on the condition that his service was to good code practices alone.
